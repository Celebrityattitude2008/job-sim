Welcome to JobSim!

Experience the ups and downs of navigating the Nigerian job market in 30 intense days. Make smart decisions, manage your resources, and survive the trenches!

1

Create Your Account

Sign up with your name, email, and password. Choose your field of study carefully — it determines your scenarios and challenges.

Available Fields:
  • 💊 Medicine - Handle patient relationships & medical ethics
  • ⚙️ Engineering - Manage construction & technical projects
  • ⚖️ Law - Navigate court cases & legal dilemmas
  • 💼 Business - Build & scale your business
  • 💻 Computer Science - Code and innovate
  • 📺 Mass Communication - Media and journalism
  • 🩺 Nursing - Patient care & healthcare
  • 🌾 Agriculture - Farming & sustainability
  • 🏛️ Political Science - Politics & governance
  • 📚 Education - Teaching & learning
2

Understand Your Stats

You start with ₦150,000 and must navigate 30 days with three key metrics:

💰
Money

Your available funds. Earn and spend wisely. Running out = Game Over!

😰
Stress

Your mental health (0-100%). Too high = poor decisions. Keep it low!

📈
Growth

Your professional development. Make smart choices to increase this.

3

Make Decisions Daily

Each day presents a realistic scenario with 3 choices. Every choice impacts your stats:

📋 Example Scenario:

"Your boss wants you to work overtime without extra pay. What do you do?"

Option A:

Refuse & assert your rights

💰 -₦0 | 😰 +20 | 📈 +15
Option B:

Accept without complaint

💰 +₦5000 | 😰 +30 | 📈 +5
Option C:

Negotiate for compensation

💰 +₦3000 | 😰 -5 | 📈 +10
4

Manage Your Resources

Smart resource management is crucial:

💡 Money Tips:

Budget carefully. Don't spend all your money early. Save for emergencies. Some scenarios offer investment opportunities with big returns.

😌 Stress Management:

High stress leads to poor decisions. Look for stress-relief opportunities. Sometimes, paying a bit more for convenience reduces stress (e.g., hiring help, taking breaks).

🚀 Growth Opportunities:

Growth comes from making tough decisions and taking calculated risks. Courses, training, and professional development can boost growth but cost money.

5

Complete All 30 Days

Your goal is to survive all 30 days with:

  • ✴️ At least ₦0 (don't go broke!)
  • ✴️ Stress managed (ideally below 50%)
  • ✴️ Growth maximized (aim for 200+)

Pro tip: There's no single "right" choice. Different decisions lead to different outcomes. Your choices define your story!

6

Unlock Achievements & Compete

Complete challenges to unlock achievements:

💰 Money Master:

End with ₦200,000+

😌 Zen Master:

Keep stress ≤ 20%

🏆 Survivor:

Complete 30 days

📈 Growth King:

Achieve 300+ growth

View the Leaderboard to see how you rank against other players!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I run out of money?

Game Over! You failed to survive. Learn from your mistakes and try again. Try different strategies next time.

Can I restart if I make a bad decision?

No, but that's the challenge! Every decision matters. Your choices define your story. After completing 30 days, you can start a new game with fresh stats.

How does the leaderboard work?

Players are ranked by their Growth score first, then Money. Only completed games appear. You can filter by field of study to see how you rank among peers.

Can I change my field of study?

Once you select a field during signup, it's tied to your account. You can create a new account to try a different field.

Are the scenarios realistic?

Yes! All scenarios are inspired by real challenges in the Nigerian job market. Learn valuable lessons while having fun.

How long does a full game take?

About 15-30 minutes depending on how fast you read. You'll interact with 3 scenarios per day (1-2 minutes each).
